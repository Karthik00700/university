Research

Real work on real problems — carried out by staff and students together.

Research Centres

Our four main research areas, each with dedicated labs and funding.

Centre for Applied AI

Building AI tools that solve practical problems in healthcare, education, and agriculture — in partnership with local businesses and the NHS.

Sustainability & Land Use Lab

Studying how land, water, and energy can be managed more sustainably — with field research sites across the UK countryside.

Community Health Institute

Focused on health inequalities, mental health access, and preventive care in underserved communities across the region.

Social & Cultural Studies Group

Exploring identity, migration, memory, and belonging through qualitative research, oral history, and community partnerships.
