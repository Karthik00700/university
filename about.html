About Yoda Uni

Who we are, where we came from, and what we stand for.

Our Story

Almost 80 years of learning together.

Yoda Uni opened its doors in 1947, founded by a group of local educators who believed that higher education should be accessible to everyone in the community — not just those who could afford it or who lived near a city.

We started with 300 students and three subjects. Today we have 12,000 students across 7 schools, but the founding belief hasn't changed: education works best when people feel safe, supported, and genuinely welcomed.

Our campus sits on 90 acres of mixed woodland and open green space. We think environment matters — that a beautiful, calm place to study makes a real difference to how students feel and how well they do.
