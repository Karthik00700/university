Admissions

Everything you need to apply — explained clearly and simply.

How to Apply

Five straightforward steps.

  1. Pick a Program

    Browse the Programs page and find the course that fits what you want to study and where you want to go.

  2. Check Entry Requirements

    Each program lists the grades and qualifications we look for. If you're unsure, contact us — we're happy to talk it through.

  3. Fill In the Application

    Our online form is straightforward. It asks for your personal details, academic background, and chosen program.

  4. Upload Your Documents

    You'll need your transcripts, a reference, and a short personal statement — about one page is plenty.

  5. Wait for Our Decision

    We aim to get back to every applicant within four weeks. You'll hear from us by email.
